A portable, markdown-based card system that keeps AI conversations coherent across sessions by making decisions, questions, and structure explicit.

Organizational Philosophy

Like collectible card games separate product (the actual cards, rules, deck lists) from marketing materials (websites, promotional content) in their file organization, ŒΩœåŒ∑œÉŒπœÇ maintains clean separation of concerns.

Cards live in version-controlled /decks/ with cryptographic seeding and immutable identity. Website assets live in /site/ as presentation layer. The website can display, reference, and showcase cards - but the source files remain organized separately.

Think Magic: The Gathering - card database (organized product files) displayed on Gatherer website (presentation files), or Pok√©mon TCG card data shown on pokemon.com. Clean file structure, integrated presentation.
